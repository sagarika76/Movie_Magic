🎬 Welcome to Movie Magic

Movie Magic is your ultimate destination for booking movie tickets with ease, comfort, and a touch of magic.

🌟 What is Movie Magic?

  • 🎟️ Easy Ticket Booking: Choose your favorite movie and book seats instantly.
  • 📅 Real-time Scheduling: Check live show timings at your nearest theaters.
  • 💺 Seat Selection: Pick seats of your choice from an interactive seating layout.
  • 🔒 Secure Payments: Multiple payment options with strong encryption and QR support.
  • 📧 Instant Confirmation: Receive confirmation and ticket details immediately.
  • 🧾 PDF Ticket Generation: Download and save your digital ticket.

🚀 Why Choose Movie Magic?

  • ✅ Fast and user-friendly interface.
  • ✅ No registration hassles – easy session-based login.
  • ✅ Real-time database for managing user bookings.
  • ✅ Voice and face-based emotion recognition integration.
  • ✅ Personalized dashboard to view your history.

📂 Technologies Used

  • 🐍 Python + Flask for backend
  • 🎨 HTML, CSS for UI
  • 🗄️ SQLite / DynamoDB for storage
  • 📤 AWS SNS for email notifications
  • 🧠 Machine Learning for emotion prediction

🧙‍♂️ The Magic Behind the Scenes

  1. User selects a movie and theater.
  2. Seat layout is displayed dynamically.
  3. Once seats are selected, payment screen is shown with a QR code.
  4. Payment confirmation saves booking to database.
  5. Ticket is generated, styled, and shown with options to download.
